About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Nawab's Rhapsody was born out of a shared love for the art of cooking and a deep appreciation for diverse flavors. Abhijith Shaju , Abhinav and Ambika Raj decided to bring their vision to life by crafting a restaurant that celebrates the rich tapestry of world cuisine. With a dream in one hand and a chef's knife in the other, our culinary journey began.

Our Mission

At Nawab's Rhapsody, our mission is to delight your taste buds with the finest ingredients, elevate the dining experience, and create moments that linger long after your meal ends. We believe in the power of food to connect people, and our commitment to quality, creativity, and sustainability is unwavering.

Culinary Excellence

Our kitchen is the heart of our establishment, where our talented culinary team crafts dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. We take pride in using locally-sourced, fresh ingredients to create culinary masterpieces that honor tradition while embracing innovation. From classic favorites to inventive creations, every dish that leaves our kitchen is a labor of love.

Meet Our Chef

Sanjeev Kapoor, our head chef, is the creative force behind our extraordinary menu. With a National Award of ‘Best Chef of India’.Chef Sanjeev Kapoor is a renowned Indian chef, entrepreneur, and television personality who has made significant contributions to the culinary world. Born on April 10, 1964, in Haryana, India, Kapoor is widely regarded as one of the most celebrated chefs in the country. He is known for his expertise in Indian cuisine and has played a pivotal role in popularizing it globally.

Kunal Kapoor brings an unparalleled level of expertise to our restaurant. Their passion for culinary artistry is evident in every dish that graces your table.Chef Kunal Kapoor is a renowned Indian celebrity chef, restaurateur, and television personality. Born on September 18, 1979, in Mumbai, India, he has made significant contributions to the culinary world. Chef Kapoor is widely recognized for his charming presence on various cooking shows, where he shares his expertise and passion for food.

The Dining Experience

Step into Nawab’s Rhapsody, and you'll be greeted by an ambiance that is as inviting as it is elegant. With dimly lit, rustic decor, and exposed brick walls, our establishment exudes a warm and inviting charm. Soft jazz melodies waft through the air, creating a soothing backdrop for conversation. Tables are elegantly set with fine linens and gleaming cutlery, offering a touch of refinement to your dining experience. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a romantic dinner, or gathering with friends and family, our restaurant is the perfect backdrop for any moment.
